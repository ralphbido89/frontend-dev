Sacramento

Tourist Spots

Sacramento is a gorgeous city located in Northern California and is the state's capital.

It is home to urban living accompanied with green sceneries and mountains.

Sacramento is also the center for California's healthcare industry and educational Hub.

Notable institutions in Sacramento are UC Davis and Cal State University.
