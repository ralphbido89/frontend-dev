San Diego

Tourist Spots

San Diego is a vibrant city located in the Pacific coast of Southern California.

It is known for its stunning beaches, coves and amazing culture.

San Diego also has delicious food ranging in diverse types from Mexican to Asian cuisines.

Notable tourist spots are Pacific Beach, La Jolla Cove and Gaslamp Quarters
